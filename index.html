I am an undergraduate student in the Department of Electrical & Computer Engineering at the University of Washington. My academic background draws broadly from theoretical and algorithmic aspects of signal and image processing, machine learning, control systems, and numerical optimization, with applications to perception, planning, and control in robotics.

In my free time, I participate in STEM education and outreach. Occasionally, I also play music, arrange music, and draw stuff.
