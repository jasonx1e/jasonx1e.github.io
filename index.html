I am a final-year student in the Department of Electrical & Computer Engineering at the University of Washington, currently working with Prof. Xu Chen in the Department of Mechanical Engineering. My background draws broadly from robotics, computer vision, machine (deep) learning, signal processing, control systems, and numerical optimization. I am interested in building closed-loop autonomous systems that can perceive and interact with the physical world.

In my free time, I participate in STEM education and outreach. Occasionally, I also play music, arrange music, and draw stuff.
